<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon Tier List: Best Classes for Beginners">

In **echoes of aincrad online**, your weapon is your class. There are six primary weapon types available at launch, each with distinct playstyles, Sword Skills, and defensive capabilities. Choosing the right weapon early on determines how you handle boss encounters and group pulls.

| Tier | Weapon Type | Role | Shield Access | Mobility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S** | **Sword and Shield** | Balanced Starter | Yes | Balanced |
| **A** | **Rapier** | Fast Reach/Chains | Yes | High |
| **A** | **Mace** | Stagger/Breaker | Yes | Medium |
| **B** | **Two-Handed Sword** | Charged Heavy | No | Low |
| **B** | **Dagger** | Quick Pressure | No | Very High |
| **B** | **Two-Handed Axe** | Area Damage | No | Low |

**S-Tier: Sword and Shield**
This is the tutorial weapon and arguably the most powerful for solo play. It allows you to block heavy attacks while maintaining a steady flow of Sword Skills. The "Reversal Slash" mechanic is easiest to perform with this set, providing a safe window to counter-attack after a successful guard.

**A-Tier: Rapier and Mace**
The Rapier excels at maintaining distance while delivering rapid-fire thrusts. It is the best choice for players who enjoy "Switch Mode" tactics with partners. The Mace, conversely, is the king of stagger. If a boss has a high defense bar, the Mace can break through it faster than any other weapon, opening a window for massive Combination Skills.

<Callout type="success" title="Weapon Mastery">
You earn Weapon Exp by using a specific type in combat. This unlocks up to three equipable Sword Skills. Even if you prefer one weapon, it is recommended to test all types in the demo version to see which flow matches your reaction time.
</Callout>

**B-Tier: Two-Handed Variants and Daggers**
The Two-Handed Sword and Axe offer the highest raw damage but come with a significant risk: you cannot use a shield. Your dodge becomes a slower roll, making positioning and timing critical. Daggers are extremely fast but require you to stay in close range, which can be suicidal in high-level dungeons without perfect parry timing.

</Section>

For the ultimate challenge, **echoes of aincrad online** offers the Death Game Mode. This mode is designed to simulate the "permadeath" stakes of the original SAO incident. It is not for the faint of heart, as a single mistake can lead to the loss of dozens of hours of progress.

<Callout type="warning" title="High Stakes Warning">
In Death Game Mode, your preparation at the Town of Beginnings is more important than your combat skill. Always carry the maximum amount of Healing Potions and SP Potions. If your health drops below 30%, use Switch Mode immediately and retreat to a Safety Area.
</Callout>

While the risks are high, the rewards are purely for prestige and personal satisfaction. There are no exclusive power-ups tied to this mode, ensuring that players who prefer a standard JRPG experience don't miss out on essential content. However, clearing the game in Death Game Mode is expected to be the rarest achievement for the 2026 gaming season.
