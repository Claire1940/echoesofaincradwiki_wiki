<Section icon={Swords} title="Echoes of Aincrad Coop: Launch Status & Multiplayer Reality">

At launch, Echoes of Aincrad is a dedicated, single-player action JRPG. Despite the "Online" legacy of the Sword Art Online franchise, developer Game Studio Inc. has focused resources on delivering a highly polished offline survival experience where you control a fully customizable protagonist. 

Instead of playing with other human players over the network, you tackle the dangerous floors of Aincrad alongside an AI-controlled companion. This design is officially known as the **Switch co-op system**, which mimics the tag-team tactical dynamics seen in the original anime series.

| Feature | Launch Status | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Online Multiplayer** | **Not Available** | No native online lobby or network matchmaking at launch. |
| **Local Couch Co-op** | **Not Available** | The game does not support split-screen or shared controller play. |
| **Switch Co-op System** | **Fully Supported** | Advanced AI partner companion system with real-time commands. |
| **Future Online Plans** | **Conditional** | Developers stated they may add online co-op if requested post-launch. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Multiplayer Misconceptions">
Many players confuse *Echoes of Aincrad* with other multiplayer-focused SAO titles like *Fractured Daydream*. This game is a self-contained action JRPG focused on survival, personal builds, and AI partner synergy.

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the AI Switch Co-op System">

While you cannot queue up with real-world friends, the AI-driven companion mechanics are highly sophisticated. You do not fight alone; you choose a dedicated partner before heading out on quests, managing their equipment, weapons, and behavioral patterns.

### Tactical Modes Explained

Your partner can be commanded in real time using the tactical action wheel. Choosing the right mode dictates how your team manages aggro, splits enemy groups, and survives boss rooms.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Switch Mode (Aggro Control)">
    - **Draws enemy focus** away from the player character.
    - Provides crucial breathing room to heal or charge heavy attacks.
    - Best used when your stamina is depleted or during boss rage phases.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Free Mode (Focused Assault)">
    - **Coordinates attacks** on a single target to maximize stagger.
    - Automatically helps clear smaller mob groups efficiently.
    - Best used when trying to break enemy defenses or burst down bosses.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Bonus">
Always pair your weapon with a complementary partner. If you run a high-damage, shieldless weapon like the Two-Handed Sword, bring a partner with a Mace or Shield (such as Wyzeman) to draw aggro and absorb incoming hits.
</Callout>

### Support & Combination Skills
During combat, you can manually trigger your partner's **Support Skills** via the action wheel to receive heals, attack buffs, or protective shields. Additionally, dealing consistent damage builds up a team meter, allowing you to unleash devastating **Combination Skills**—joint cinematic attacks that deal massive stagger damage and can turn the tide of difficult boss encounters.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Optimize Team Synergy">

To make your AI partner feel like a true co-op companion, you must actively invest in their progression. They do not automatically scale to keep up with your character; they require manual upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Matching Gear">
    Visit the Smithy in the Town of Beginnings to craft and upgrade weapons specifically for your companion. Keeping their weapon tier on par with yours is vital for maintaining damage output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Growth Points">
    When your Cardinal Rank rises, check your partner's stats at the Inn. Allocate points to attributes that directly scale their weapon type (e.g., Strength for Zash, Dexterity for Argo).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Complementary EX-MODs">
    Apply EX-MOD effects to your partner's gear. Giving them stamina-recovery modifiers or health-regen perks ensures they remain active and alive during prolonged dungeon crawls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Survival Tip">
In high-stakes environments like the Death Game Mode, your partner's survival directly impacts yours. If your partner falls, you lose access to Switch commands, healing supports, and Combination Skills, doubling the pressure on your character.
</Callout>

</Section>
