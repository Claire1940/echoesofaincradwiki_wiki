<Section icon={Shield} title="Is Echoes of Aincrad Multiplayer? Official Launch Status">

Many players diving into the virtual world of Aincrad expect a fully functional MMORPG experience matching the original anime lore. However, **echoes of aincrad multiplayer** features are not available at launch, as the game is structured as a dedicated **single-player action JRPG**. 

Instead of grouping up with real-world players, your journey through the floating castle is a highly personalized solo endeavor. You design your custom protagonist, choose your weapon style, and rely on an offline partner system to clear floors and survive the death game.

<Section icon={Swords} title="How the AI Partner System Replaces Online Co-Op">

These partners act as your "co-op" teammates. Each companion features a distinct weapon archetype, a unique set of Support Skills, and cinematic Combination Skills that can turn the tide of battle.

<Section icon={Target} title="Real-Time Tactical Commands and Switch Mechanics">

Even without a real human player by your side, you can coordinate complex tactical maneuvers during real-time combat. The game features two distinct tactical modes and a signature mechanic that mimics the anime's team-based combat flow.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Tactical Mode">
    Toggle between **Switch Mode** to split an aggressive enemy's attention, or **Free Mode** to focus your combined damage output on a single target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger Support Skills">
    Open the quick-access action wheel in battle to manually command your partner to execute healing, defensive buffs, or crowd-control spells.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute a Perfect Switch">
    Time your dodge or parry perfectly to stagger the enemy, then execute a **Reversal Slash** to swap positions with your partner and unleash a devastating counterattack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Combination Skills">
    Once your joint combat meter is fully charged, activate your equipped Combination Skill to trigger a highly cinematic, high-damage dual assault.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Advantage">
Using the Switch mechanic is not just flashy; it temporarily pauses your Stamina consumption, allowing you to recover resources while your partner takes the frontline.
</Callout>
