<Section icon={Shield} title="Is Echoes of Aincrad Multiplayer at Launch?">

At its global release on July 10, 2026, **echoes of aincrad is multiplayer** only in spirit, launching strictly as a **single-player action JRPG**. While the Sword Art Online universe is famously based on a massive multiplayer online death game, developer Game Studio Inc. designed the core release around a solo progression loop. Players create their own customizable protagonist and explore the floating castle of Aincrad alongside an AI-controlled partner rather than real-world friends. 

To help you understand how the launch version compares to a traditional multiplayer game, the table below breaks down the game's core connectivity and party systems:

| Feature | Launch Status | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Online Co-Op** | Not Available | You cannot invite friends or matchmake with other players at launch. |
| **Local Split-Screen** | Not Available | There is no local couch co-op support for console or PC players. |
| **AI Partner System** | Available | Choose from 6 unique NPC companions to fight alongside you in combat. |
| **Offline 'Switch' Co-Op** | Available | Swap aggro and coordinate combat strategies with your companion in real time. |

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Offline 'Switch' Co-Op System Explained">

Even though you cannot team up with real players online, the game simulates a cooperative dynamic through its **Switch co-op system**. This mechanic mirrors the iconic \"Switch\" tactic from the Sword Art Online anime, allowing you to manage combat flow, recover resources, and execute high-damage combinations with your AI companion.

You can swap between two primary tactical modes in real time to suit your combat needs:

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Switch Mode">
    - **Draws enemy focus** to your AI partner immediately.
    - Gives your avatar **breathing room** to fall back.
    - Allows you to safely **recover stamina** and SP.
    - Sets up powerful **Combination Skills**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Free Mode">
    - AI partner fights **nearby targets** automatically.
    - Does not focus on your current target.
    - Ideal for **clearing trash mobs** and groups.
    - Helps manage **mob density** in dungeons.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

To make the most of this offline system, you must build synergy with your chosen companion. There are six primary partner characters available, each specializing in a distinct role:

| Partner | Weapon Type | Combat Role | Best Strategic Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Iori** | Sword & Shield | Healing & Support | Keeps you alive during high-stakes boss battles. |
| **Wyzeman** | Mace & Shield | Damage & Stagger | Breaks enemy guards and creates opening windows. |
| **Argo** | Dagger | Scouting & Speed | Quick pressure and locating hidden dungeon pathways. |
| **Zash** | Two-Handed Sword | Defensive Buffs | Provides damage mitigation when taking heavy hits. |
| **Stina** | Specialized | Weakness Exploitation | Maximizes damage against elemental or typing vulnerabilities. |
| **Musoh** | Specialized | Crowd Control | Groups up targets and keeps mobs off your back. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Tip">
Match your partner to your weapon build. If you are using a slow, high-damage Two-Handed Sword, bring Iori or Musoh to keep enemies distracted and your health topped off.
</Callout>
