<Section icon={Target} title="How to Carry Over Save Data from the Demo">

If you played the official *Echoes of Aincrad* demo during Steam Next Fest or downloaded it on consoles, your progression can be carried directly into the full retail version. This includes your custom avatar, unlocked skills, and items gathered across the five available beta missions. Follow these steps to complete the transfer:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Update the Client">
    Before starting, ensure both your demo save file is still on your system storage and your full retail game is updated to the latest day-one patch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Launch and Accept Terms">
    Open the full version of *Echoes of Aincrad*. Proceed through the initial language selections and accept the user agreements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Prompt">
    The game will automatically scan your system storage for existing demo data. When the "Carry Over Save Data" prompt appears, select **Yes**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and Load">
    Press and hold **Confirm** on the transfer screen. Once complete, select **Game Start** from the main menu and load your transferred save slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Death Game Mode Limitation">
