<Section icon={Target} title="Character Progression and Customization Guide">

In Echoes of Aincrad, you are not tethered to the path of Kirito. Instead, you create your own custom avatar, allowing for a personalized journey through the death game. Progression is handled through a combination of traditional leveling and a flexible attribute allocation system.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Avatar Creation">
    Customize your visual appearance and initial build. This identity stays with you throughout the 100-floor climb, serving as your unique legend in the world of Aincrad.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Attribute Allocation">
    Every level provides Growth Points. You must decide whether to invest in Strength for raw damage, Agility for evasion, or Vitality for survival. Reaching certain thresholds unlocks passive buffs like increased attack speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Weapon Modification">
    Visit the town blacksmith to dismantle unwanted loot. You can transfer passive abilities from one weapon to another, allowing you to craft the "perfect" gear set without relying purely on RNG drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Partner Synergy">
    Choose a combat partner (like Yori or Sayu) to accompany you. Deepening your bond unlocks new tactical options and defensive barriers that can save you in critical situations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Growth Point Tips">
Focusing on a single attribute early on can unlock powerful Tier 1 passives faster. For example, high Agility provides a significant boost to XP gains, which can speed up the early-game grind.
</Callout>
