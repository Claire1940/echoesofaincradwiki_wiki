<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Xbox Setup & Demo Progress Transfer">

If you played the free demo version on Xbox Series X|S, you can carry your progress over to the full retail release. Follow these exact steps to ensure your save data transfers correctly without any loss of progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Keep the Demo Installed">
    Do not delete the Echoes of Aincrad demo save files from your Xbox storage before initiating the transfer. The full version needs to detect the local save database.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Update the Full Game">
    Launch the full version of Echoes of Aincrad on your Xbox. Ensure your console is connected to the network and has downloaded the latest day-one patch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Accept the Agreements">
    Proceed through the initial boot screens and accept the user agreements. The game will automatically scan your Xbox storage for demo data.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm the Import Prompt">
    When the "Carry over" prompt appears on your screen, press and hold the **Confirm** button. Once the success message displays, select **Close** and choose **Game Start** to load your transferred save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Death Game Mode Save Restriction">
Save data carried over from the Xbox demo cannot be converted into Death Game Mode. If you wish to play the permadeath mode, you must create a fresh save file.
</Callout>
