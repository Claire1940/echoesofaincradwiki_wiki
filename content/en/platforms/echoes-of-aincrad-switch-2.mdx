<Section icon={Target} title="How to Setup and Transfer Demo Progress">

If you have already spent hours in the beta phase demo, you can carry your progress into the full game. This ensures you don't have to recreate your avatar or re-clear the initial five missions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Update the Full Game">
    Before launching the retail version, ensure all day-one patches are installed. The transfer tool requires the latest version to recognize demo save files.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify Platform Consistency">
    Save data transfer only works within the same ecosystem (e.g., PS5 Demo to PS5 Full Game). Cross-platform transfer is not supported at launch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Carry Over">
    Launch the full game. When the prompt "Carry over demo save data?" appears, select "Yes." You must press and hold the confirm button to finalize the process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Load and Sync">
    Once the transfer is complete, select "Game Start" and choose your transferred save slot. Your items, Col, and Cardinal Rank will be exactly as you left them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Death Game Exception">
Note that demo save data **cannot** be converted into a Death Game Mode file. If you wish to play the permadeath mode, you must start a fresh save file.
</Callout>

</Section>
