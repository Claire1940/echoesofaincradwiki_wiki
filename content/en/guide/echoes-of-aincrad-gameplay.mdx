### Stamina and SP Resource Management

Success on the frontlines requires balancing two primary combat resources: Stamina and SP. Standard actions such as sprinting, dodging, and guarding with a shield drain your Stamina bar. Unleashing powerful weapon-specific Sword Skills consumes SP. Over-committing to heavy attacks will leave you completely vulnerable, making button-mashing a quick path to defeat.

### Defensive Options and Counterattacks

Echoes of Aincrad separates itself from standard hack-and-slash games with its depth of defensive maneuvers. Depending on your equipped loadout, you have access to three primary defensive tools:

| Defense Type | Input Requirement | Best Used For | Tactical Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perfect Dodge** | Precise timing on dodge roll | Unblockable heavy boss attacks | Grants temporary invincibility frames |
| **Shield Guard** | Hold block button with shield | Sustained physical strikes | Minimizes direct health damage |
| **Parry / Reversal** | Tight timing window on block | Standard enemy weapon swings | Breaks enemy stance and triggers partner combo |

### Core Survival Rules

- **Permanent Defeat**: If your character's health drops to zero in this mode, your character save file is permanently deleted.
- **No Respawning**: There are no second chances, revival items, or checkpoint rollbacks.
- **Safe Disconnects**: Exiting the game normally, returning to the main menu, or experiencing a sudden power outage will not delete your save data. The penalty only triggers upon actual in-game defeat.
- **Unlock Requirements**: Standard edition players unlock this mode after completing the main story campaign. Deluxe and Ultimate Edition owners receive an **Early Unlock** to risk their reality from the very first playthrough.
