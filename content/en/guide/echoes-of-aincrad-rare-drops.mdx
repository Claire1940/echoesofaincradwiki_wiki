<Section icon={Sparkles} title="X-Mods: The Core of Build Customization">

X-mods are the most dynamic aspect of the **echoes of aincrad rare drops** system. These are not static items but randomized modifiers that can be fused to your weaponry. This allows for a degree of flexibility that is rarely seen in other ARPGs. You can continually reshape your avatar's performance by transferring these perks at a blacksmith.

| X-Mod Type | Effect | Rarity | Recommended Build |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Stamina Saver** | Reduces stamina cost per swing | Rare | Heavy Weapons (Axes/Maces) |
| **SP Catalyst** | Increases SP charge rate on parry | Epic | Skill-heavy builds |
| **Haste Pulse** | Boosts overall attack speed | Rare | Fast weapons (Rapiers) |
| **Guardian Veil** | Adds a small defensive barrier | Epic | Tank/Solo builds |
| **Vampiric Edge** | Heals a % of damage dealt | Ultra Rare | High-risk glass cannons |

The ability to transfer these mods means that even if you find a low-level weapon with a high-tier X-mod, the drop is still incredibly valuable. You can take that "Vampiric Edge" and move it to your end-game legendary sword once you reach the top of the floor.

<Callout type="info" title="Blacksmithing Tip">
The cost of transferring X-mods increases with the rarity of the mod. Save your Col for the most impactful transfers rather than wasting it on minor stat bumps early in the game.
</Callout>
