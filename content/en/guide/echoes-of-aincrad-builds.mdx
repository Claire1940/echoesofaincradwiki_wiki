<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics and Defense Optimization">

While damage is king in **echoes of aincrad builds**, survival on higher difficulties like Death Game Mode requires understanding defensive frames. The game utilizes a precise dodge and parry system. For players struggling with timing, the Wind Fleur-de-lis (Rapier) offers a slight advantage by increasing the invincibility window during dodges by approximately 3 to 5 frames at 60 FPS.

**Key Defensive Tactics:**
- **Animation Canceling**: Use your shield to cancel the recovery frames of normal attacks, allowing for faster subsequent swings.
- **SP Management**: SP is restored by successfully parrying or dealing damage. Avoid weapons that only offer "Flat SP" restoration as they are redundant.
- **Stamina Management**: Rapiers are the only weapons that allow for infinite stamina loops. For all other builds, you must back off once the stamina bar is depleted.

| Defense Type | Frame Window | Benefit | Risk |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perfect Dodge** | ~6-10 Frames | Slows time/Free hit | High timing demand |
| **Parry** | ~8-12 Frames | Massive SP gain | Requires Shield |
| **Standard Block** | Continuous | Reduces damage | Drains Stamina |

<Callout type="info" title="Fear Status Effect">
Be wary of enemies that inflict "Fear." This status prevents you from attacking, though you can still dodge. Most weapons offering "Fear Resistance" are currently ineffective; it is better to dodge the initial fear-inducing shout than to rely on gear resistance.
</Callout>
