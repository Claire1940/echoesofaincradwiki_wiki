There are six distinct weapon categories in the current build of the game. Each weapon offers a unique playstyle, ranging from the rapid strikes of the Dagger to the heavy, AOE-focused swings of the Two-Handed Axe. <Section icon={Sparkles} title="Stats and Proficiency Progression">

Unlocking more **Echoes of Aincrad sword skills** requires a dedicated focus on Weapon Proficiency. While your character level provides Growth Points for base stats, your skill effectiveness is determined by how you distribute those points. Different weapons scale with different attributes, making it essential to align your build with your preferred weapon.

| Stat Name | Primary Benefit | Scaling Weapons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength (STR)** | Raw Physical Damage | 2H Sword, 2H Axe, Mace |
| **Dexterity (DEX)** | Skill Crit & Accuracy | Rapier, 1H Sword |
| **Agility (AGI)** | Speed & Evasion | Dagger, Rapier |
| **Intelligence (INT)** | SP Ability Effectiveness | Special Weapon Mods |

Your **Cardinal Rank** also plays a massive role in your progression. As this rank increases, merchants will offer better gear, and the Smithy will unlock higher-tier crafting recipes. However, be warned: the world levels with you. A higher Cardinal Rank means monsters will become more aggressive and resilient.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Every action—light attacks, heavy attacks, and Sword Skills—consumes **Stamina (SP)**. Running out of SP mid-combo leaves you vulnerable to enemy counters.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Combat Mechanics">

Mastering **Echoes of Aincrad sword skills** involves more than just pressing buttons. You must understand the trade-offs between defense and mobility. The game offers a unique "Sword and Shield" system that changes how your character moves.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Shield Loadout">
    - **Increased Defense**: Block incoming attacks to mitigate damage.
    - **Reduced Mobility**: Dash distance is cut by 50%.
    - **Weapon Compatibility**: Can be used with 1H Sword, Rapier, and Mace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Two-Handed Loadout">
    - **Maximized Damage**: High scaling and AOE capabilities.
    - **Enhanced Evasion**: The dash maneuver is replaced by a high-iframe roll.
    - **Weapon Compatibility**: 2H Sword and 2H Axe only.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The choice of weapon also dictates your defensive maneuver. While light weapons allow for quick dashes, heavy weapons like the Two-Handed Sword force your character into a dodge roll. This roll covers more ground but has a different timing for invincibility frames compared to the standard dash.

| Weapon Type | Defensive Move | Shield Compatible? | Range |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1H Sword | Dash | Yes | Medium |
| Rapier | Dash | Yes | Long (Thrust) |
| Dagger | Dash | No | Short |
| 2H Sword | Roll | No | Long (Sweep) |
| 2H Axe | Roll | No | Medium (AOE) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Switch Mode">
Use **Switch Mode** to have your AI partner draw enemy aggro. This gives you the breathing room to charge up skills like Cascade or recover your SP.
</Callout>
