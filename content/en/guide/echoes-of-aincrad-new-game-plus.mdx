<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Build Optimization: Weapons and EX-MODs">

Surviving your post-game runs requires a deep understanding of the weapon system. In this game, your equipped weapon dictates your active class and skill set. You must choose weapon types that offer defensive utility and pair them with high-tier EX-MOD effects at the blacksmith.

### Weapon Type Overview

*   **Sword & Shield**: The most balanced option. Offers active blocking and high defensive stats, making it the safest choice for permadeath runs.
*   **Rapier**: Fast chain attacks. Can be paired with a shield, allowing you to maintain high mobility while keeping your guard up.
*   **Dagger**: High attack speed and ranged throwing options. Great for hit-and-run tactics, but lacks block capabilities.
*   **Mace**: Specialized in staggering and breaking enemy guards. Excellent for control, and can be paired with a shield.
*   **Two-Handed Axe**: Massive area-of-effect damage but slow recovery times. High risk in permadeath since you cannot use a shield.
*   **Two-Handed Sword**: Slow, charged attacks. Replaces your standard dodge with a heavy roll. Only recommended for highly experienced players.

### Recommended EX-MOD Priorities

When synthesizing and upgrading your weapons at the Smithy, prioritize defensive and recovery stats to offset the threat of permadeath.

| EX-MOD Type | Priority | Core Effect | Why It Is Essential |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **HP+** | Tier S | Increases Max Health | Prevents getting one-shot by high-floor boss ultimate attacks. |
| **Damage Taken-** | Tier S | Flat Damage Reduction | Mitigates chip damage during prolonged dungeon crawls. |
| **HP Regen on Guard** | Tier A | Restores HP when blocking | Allows you to safely recover health without burning potions. |
| **Extra Attack Damage+** | Tier B | Boosts offensive output | Shortens boss fights, reducing the window for fatal mistakes. |
