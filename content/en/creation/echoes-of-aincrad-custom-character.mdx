<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Stats and Growth Points Management">

Progression for your **echoes of aincrad custom character** is handled through Growth Points (GP). Every time you level up, you receive a set amount of GP that must be manually assigned while resting at the Inn in the Town of Beginnings. Understanding the relationship between stats and weapon performance is the difference between a struggling survivor and a front-line clearer.

The seven core attributes influence everything from your raw damage to your ability to survive a boss's ultimate attack. Because you cannot easily reset stats in the early game, a focused build is highly recommended over a "jack-of-all-trades" approach.

| Attribute | Primary Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength (STR)** | Increases physical attack power and carry weight | Two-Handed Swords, Axes, and Maces |
| **Dexterity (DEX)** | Improves critical hit rate and accuracy | Rapiers, Daggers, and Bows |
| **Agility (AGI)** | Increases movement speed and dodge window | Light armor users and rapid-attack builds |
| **Intelligence (INT)** | Boosts Sword Skill damage and SP recovery | Skill-spamming builds and support roles |
| **Vitality (VIT)** | Increases maximum Health Points (HP) | Tanks and Death Game Mode survivors |
| **Endurance (END)** | Improves Stamina recovery and Guard strength | Shield users (Sword & Shield, Mace) |
| **Mind (MND)** | Enhances resistance to status effects | High-floor exploration and boss encounters |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Distribution Warning">
Do not ignore **Vitality** if you are playing in **Death Game Mode**. While high damage is tempting, a single mistimed dodge can lead to character deletion if your HP pool is too shallow.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Character Setup Guide">

Setting up your **echoes of aincrad custom character** for long-term success requires a plan. Follow these steps to ensure you don't waste Growth Points or resources during the first ten floors of Aincrad.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Beta Prologue">
    Play through the initial missions as the default avatar. This teaches you the basics of Stamina management and SP usage. Pay attention to which weapon feels most natural to your playstyle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define Your Visual Identity">
    Once the full creator unlocks, finalize your look. Remember that armor appearance can be toggled, but your base physical features are constant. Save your preset if you plan on making multiple characters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Starting Weapon">
    Visit the Town of Beginnings merchants. If you want safety, pick the Sword and Shield. If you want to clear groups quickly, the Two-Handed Axe is superior. Your choice here dictates your first 10 levels of stat growth.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Allocate Initial Growth Points">
    Don't hoard your GP. Every 5 levels, return to the Inn and boost your primary damage stat (STR or DEX) and your survival stat (VIT). A 2:1 ratio is generally recommended for the first 20 floors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Equip Your First Sword Skills">
    As you gain Weapon Exp, you will unlock Sword Skills. Equip them in your action wheel immediately. Practice the timing for "Reversal Slashes" to maximize your damage output during enemy openings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
