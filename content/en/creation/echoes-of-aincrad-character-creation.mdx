### Tactical Commands and Team Modes

To maximize partner effectiveness in combat, you must actively toggle between tactical modes using the action wheel [00首页信息-2.md]:

- **Switch Mode**: Commands your partner to draw immediate enemy aggression, giving your custom character breathing room to heal or charge heavy attacks [00首页信息-1.md, 00首页信息-2.md].
- **Free Mode**: Instructs your partner to coordinate attacks on your target or split up to efficiently clear groups of minor enemies [00首页信息-1.md, 00首页信息-2.md].
- **Combination Skills**: High-damage joint strikes unleashed once your team builds up enough combat momentum [00首页信息-2.md].
