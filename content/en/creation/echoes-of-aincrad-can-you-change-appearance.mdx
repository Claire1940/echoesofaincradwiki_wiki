<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Echoes of Aincrad Can You Change Appearance?">

When diving into Bandai Namco's highly anticipated action JRPG, one of the first questions players ask is: **echoes of aincrad can you change appearance**? The answer is a mix of deep lore immersion and strict mechanical limitations. Unlike traditional JRPGs that allow you to change your face or hairstyle at any local barber shop, this game takes a page directly from the Sword Art Online source material. 

During the initial phase of your adventure, you are locked into a default beta tester avatar. Once the official "Death Game" begins, your character undergoes a forced transformation to reveal their "real-world" self. Once you customize this real-world avatar and finalize your settings, **your physical appearance is locked permanently**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Irreversible Decisions">
Take your time in the character creator. Your physical body type, face shape, skin tone, and eye structure cannot be altered after you confirm them. Only your equipped gear, weapons, and outfits will change your visual look from that point forward.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Character Customization">

If you played the demo version, you might have been confused by the complete lack of character customization options. The demo only allows you to play as a preset default character. This is a deliberate design choice by Game Studio Inc. to represent the final "Closed Beta" phase of Sword Art Online. 

To unlock the character creator and establish your permanent identity in Aincrad, you must progress through the opening hours of the full retail game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Beta Prologue">
    Begin the main story mode. You will play through the introductory quests using the default beta-tester avatar assigned to you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear The Violet Fencer II Quest">
    Progress through the initial Floor 1 campaign until you receive and successfully complete the main story quest titled **The Violet Fencer II**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Hand Mirror Event">
    Following the quest, Akihiko Kayaba's global announcement event will trigger, trapping all players in the death game. Your character will receive the Hand Mirror item, which forces your avatar to transform.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Design Your Permanent Hero">
    The character customization suite will open automatically. Use the sliders to design your permanent physical appearance, select your starting weapon class, and confirm your name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Naming Restrictions">
Choose your name wisely. You cannot use existing canonical character names (such as Kirito or Asuna) for your custom protagonist.
</Callout>

</Section>
