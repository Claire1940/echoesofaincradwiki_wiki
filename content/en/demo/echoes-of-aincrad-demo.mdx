<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Save Data Transfer Guide">

Transferring your progress from the **echoes of aincrad demo** to the full retail version is a straightforward process, but it requires following specific steps to ensure no data is lost. Bandai Namco has confirmed that all mission progress, items gathered, and character customizations will carry over, provided the transfer occurs within the same platform ecosystem.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Update the Full Game">
    Ensure your full version of Echoes of Aincrad is updated to the latest version. Connect your console or PC to the internet to download any Day One patches required for save compatibility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Keep Demo Data Intact">
    Do not delete the demo application or its saved data from your storage device until the transfer is confirmed. The full game needs to detect the existing local save files to initiate the migration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Launch and Confirm">
    Start the full game. After accepting the initial user agreements, a prompt will appear detecting your demo progress. Select the **Carry Over** option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Transfer">
    On the confirmation screen, press and hold the confirm button. Once the "Transfer Successful" message appears, you can safely load your save from the main menu and continue from where the demo ended.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Platform Restrictions">
Save data transfer is strictly locked to the same platform. You cannot move a Steam demo save to a PlayStation 5 full version. Ensure you play the demo on the device you intend to use for the final game.
</Callout>

</Section>
