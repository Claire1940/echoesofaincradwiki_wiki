<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: A Surprising Soulslike Shift">

The combat in this title deviates significantly from fast-paced, arcade-style anime games, leaning instead toward a simplified Soulslike experience. Every light and heavy attack, dodge, and block consumes stamina. Reckless button-mashing will quickly leave your character exhausted and vulnerable to counterattacks. Perfect parries feel incredibly satisfying to land, rewarding precise defensive play with brief moments of enemy stagger.

| Combat Mechanic | Input & Resource Cost | Tactical Function & Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal Attacks** | Stamina (Low) | Standard combo chains used to build SP and pressure basic mobs. |
| **Perfect Parry** | Stamina (Medium) | Negates incoming damage and staggers opponents, opening them up to critical strikes. |
| **Sword Skills** | SP (High) | High-damage weapon-specific skills assigned to your action wheel. |
| **Dodge Roll** | Stamina (Medium) | Generates brief invincibility frames; changes to a heavy roll with two-handed weapons. |

The game also features a weapon proficiency system where using a specific weapon type earns Weapon Exp, which gradually unlocks new Sword Skills. While the core loop is highly engaging, the demo does exhibit some floaty animations and inconsistent hitboxes, meaning enemy attacks occasionally miss when they visually appear to connect.

<Callout type="tip" title="Stamina Management Tip">
Do not deplete your stamina bar completely. Always reserve enough stamina for a single dodge roll or shield block, especially when fighting aggressive Floor 1 monsters.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Performance & Platform Analysis: PC vs. Console">

Technically, the demo is a mixed bag. Visually, the outdoor environments of Aincrad are stunning and highly atmospheric, utilizing an art style reminiscent of *Scarlet Nexus*. However, indoor dungeons occasionally suffer from washed-out lighting and minor texture pop-in.

| Platform | Performance Profile | Known Technical Issues (Demo Build) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PlayStation 5 / Xbox** | Stable 60 FPS target | Minor frame drops in dense foliage; occasional audio sync lag during cinematic cutscenes. |
| **PC (Steam)** | Uncapped framerate | Poor mouse/keyboard camera emulation; sluggish menu navigation; lack of a standard quit button. |

The PC version currently struggles with mouse input precision. The camera movement emulates an analog controller stick rather than raw mouse input, resulting in a slightly sluggish feel. Additionally, the interface is clearly designed around controller layouts, making inventory management tedious when using a mouse.

<Callout type="info" title="Recommended Setup">
For the best experience in the current demo build, we highly recommend playing with a controller, even when running the game on PC via Steam.
</Callout>
