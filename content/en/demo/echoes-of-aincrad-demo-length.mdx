<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Transfer Demo Save Data to the Full Game">

Your hard work in the demo will not go to waste. Bandai Namco has confirmed that save data from the demo can be carried over directly to the full retail version of Echoes of Aincrad. This ensures you can skip the introductory tutorials and dive straight into new floors at launch.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Keep the Demo Installed">
    Do not delete your demo save files from your system storage. The full game requires these local files to detect your progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Update the Full Game">
    Launch the full version of Echoes of Aincrad on July 10, 2026, and ensure you have installed the latest day-one network patch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Accept the Transfer Prompt">
    When the main menu loads, the game will automatically detect your local demo save. Select "Carry over" from the pop-up prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and Load">
    Press and hold the Confirm button on the transfer screen. Once complete, select "Game Start" and load your transferred save slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Save Transfer Restrictions">
Save transfer only works within the same platform ecosystem (e.g., PS5 demo to PS5 full game). Cross-platform save transfer is not supported. Additionally, demo saves cannot be converted into the high-stakes **Death Game Mode**.
</Callout>

</Section>
