<Section icon={Swords} title="Echoes of Aincrad Greatsword Mechanics">

The **echoes of aincrad greatsword** is a premier heavy-damage weapon class designed for players who favor high risk and massive reward. While its swing animations are slower than those of one-handed blades, its staggering potential and raw damage output are unmatched. To make this weapon viable, you must understand how base stats interact with the game's progression systems.

In the early stages of the game, performance differences between basic weapons are relatively small. However, because dropped weapons from enemies start with low enhancement levels and zero modifications, progression becomes increasingly difficult as enemy levels rise.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Blacksmith Upgrades and Weapon Synthesis">

To unlock the full potential of your **echoes of aincrad greatsword**, you must utilize the blacksmith's enhancement and synthesis systems. The maximum enhancement limit for your gear increases naturally as you progress through the main story quests. 

Through synthesis, you can transfer unique weapon effects known as **EX-MODs** from one weapon to another, creating a highly customized build.

<Callout type="tip" title="Synthesis Tip">
Always save lower-tier weapons with high-quality EX-MODs. You can synthesize them into your main high-tier greatsword later to inherit their passive benefits.
</Callout>

| Upgrade Type | Required Materials | Primary Benefit | Best Used For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Enhancement** | Ore, Col | Increases raw weapon ATK | Keeping up with rising enemy levels |
| **Synthesis** | Duplicate Weapons | Transfers EX-MOD slots | Customizing passive combat perks |
| **Limit Break** | Rare Story Drops | Raises maximum upgrade level | End-game progression scaling |

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Greatsword Build Setup">

Setting up your greatsword build requires a systematic approach to ensure you do not waste valuable resources at the blacksmith. Follow this step-by-step progression to optimize your loadout.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire a Base Greatsword">
    Farm enemy drops or purchase a high-grade base weapon from the town merchant. Look for a weapon with at least two open EX-MOD slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Max Out Base Enhancement">
    Visit the blacksmith in town and upgrade the weapon's level to its current maximum limit. This ensures your base physical damage is optimized.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Synthesize EX-MODs">
    Select weapons from your inventory that possess offensive EX-MODs (such as critical rate or physical pierce) and synthesize them into your main greatsword.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Configure Partner Commands">
    Set your partner to **Switch Mode** to allow them to draw aggro while you charge your heavy greatsword attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Build Complete">
Once these steps are completed, your greatsword will deal up to 150% more damage compared to its base unupgraded state.
</Callout>

</Section>
