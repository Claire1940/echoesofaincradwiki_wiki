<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Craft & Upgrade Maces">

Once you have secured a blueprint for an **echoes of aincrad mace**, you must gather the necessary materials. Unlike swords which require high amounts of refined steel, maces often require a combination of heavy ores and sturdy wood for the handles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Blueprint">
    Follow the mission routes described in the Map section. Ensure you have cleared the specific Arc Pillar related to the zone to unlock the seal guarding the recipe chests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Raw Materials">
    Collect Iron Ore from the plateau regions and Sturdy Timber from the forest zones. For the Annealed Sledgehammer, you will also need "Annealing Fluid," which drops from water-based Arcs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Visit the Town Smithy">
    Head back to the Town of Beginnings. Use the Smithy station to combine your materials. Ensure your Crafting Level is at least Level 5 for the "Annealed" series of weapons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enhance with Gems">
    Slot "Impact Gems" into your mace to further increase its breaking potential. This is a 2026 meta-strategy for clearing the Prologue missions with S-rank efficiency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Material Tip">
If you are short on gold for materials, focus on completing "Yori's Request" repeatedly. The chests in this mission have a high drop rate for basic ores and gold pouches.
</Callout>
