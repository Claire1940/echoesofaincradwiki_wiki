<Section icon={Swords} title="Echoes of Aincrad Weapons Overview">

Unlike traditional JRPGs, *Sword Art Online: Echoes of Aincrad* features a classless progression system where your active weapon determines your role, mobility, and skill options. There is no magic in the floating castle of Aincrad; combat is entirely physical, relying on melee timing, stamina management, and powerful Sword Skills. 

As you defeat monsters using a specific weapon type, you accumulate Weapon Exp, unlocking specialized active skills. You can equip up to three Sword Skills at a time, allowing you to customize your active loadout. Note that weapon swaps are restricted during active exploration; you can only change your equipped gear at the Inn inside the Town of Beginnings.

<Section icon={Shield} title="In-Depth Weapon Class Analysis">

### 1. Sword and Shield (S-Tier)
The ultimate balanced starter weapon [00首页信息-1.md]. It is the default weapon you hold during the game's tutorial and beta phase. It allows you to block incoming damage to preserve your health bar, or drop the shield entirely to increase your movement speed and swing velocity. It is highly forgiving and fits any partner synergy [00首页信息-1.md, 00首页信息-2.md].

### 2. Rapier (A-Tier)
A high-skill, high-reward weapon that excels at rapid-fire thrust attacks and counters. It boasts exceptional forward reach, allowing you to poke at enemies from a relatively safe distance. Because it is shield-compatible, it offers an incredible blend of rapid offensive chains and instant defensive recovery.

### 3. Mace (A-Tier)
An essential utility weapon for breaking through heavily armored targets and blocking bosses. The Mace deals massive posture damage, targeting the enemy's stamina bar rather than just their health. Depleting this gauge knocks the enemy flat on the ground, opening them up to high-damage Combination Skills from you and your partner.

### 4. Dagger (B-Tier)
Designed for agile, fast-paced playstyles. Daggers hit rapidly but deal low individual damage per strike. Its unique mechanic is the ability to throw spectral blades via specific Sword Skills, giving you a reliable ranged attack option to chip away at enemies safely. However, the lack of a shield makes positioning critical.

### 5. Two-Handed Sword (B-Tier)
The premier single-target heavy hitter. Equipped with slow but devastating animations, this weapon allows you to charge up your attacks to unleash massive damage bursts. When wielding this giant blade, your standard dodge step is replaced by a heavy roll, which uses more stamina and requires precise timing.

### 6. Two-Handed Axe (B-Tier)
Built for clearing crowds of monsters. The Two-Handed Axe trades away all defensive tools in exchange for sweeping, wide-angle area-of-effect (AoE) strikes. While highly effective at clearing out groups of smaller dungeon mobs, its long swing recovery windows make it highly dangerous to use against fast-moving bosses.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Upgrade Weapons at the Smithy">

To keep your weapon competitive as your Cardinal Rank increases, you must regularly upgrade your gear at the Smithy located in the Town of Beginnings. Upgrading requires a mix of Col (gold), crafting materials found in dungeons, and duplicate weapons.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Raw Materials">
    Explore dungeons thoroughly and activate checkpoints to mark resource deposits and treasure chests on your map [00首页信息-1.md]. Collect ore, monster drops, and Col.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Blacksmith">
    Return to the Town of Beginnings and approach the Smithy [00首页信息-1.md]. Select either the crafting menu to forge a new weapon from a recipe, or the upgrade menu to boost your current weapon's base stats [00首页信息-1.md].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fuse Duplicate Weapons for EX-MODs">
    Combine two weapons of the exact same type to pool their unique passive traits. This process allows you to stack up to four **EX-MOD effects** on a single weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize EX-MOD Passives">
    Focus on unlocking EX-MODs that complement your playstyle, such as reduced Stamina consumption for heavy weapons, or increased critical hit rate for rapid-fire weapons like the Rapier [00首页信息-1.md].
  </Step>
</Steps>
