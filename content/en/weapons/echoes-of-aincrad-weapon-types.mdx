To keep your **echoes of aincrad weapon types** effective as you progress through higher floors, you must utilize the Smithy. Improving a weapon is a multi-faceted process that involves both leveling up base parameters and managing EX-Mods.

The Smithy allows you to spend specific materials or "sacrifice" unwanted weapons to increase a weapon's base damage. This is separate from your personal Weapon Proficiency. While proficiency unlocks skills, the Smithy increases the raw math behind your damage output.

| Upgrade Type | Required Resource | Primary Effect |

| **Armor Forging** | Ore / Monster Parts | Improves survivability and resistances |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Stats, Growth Points, and Cardinal Rank">

Your character's effectiveness with various **echoes of aincrad weapon types** is heavily influenced by how you spend Growth Points. These points are earned every time you level up by defeating monsters. Unlike many games where stats are automatic, Aincrad requires you to manually apply points while resting at the Inn.

| Stat Name | Primary Influence | Recommended For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength (STR)** | Physical damage / Carry weight | Two-Handed Swords, Axes, Maces |
| **Dexterity (DEX)** | Skill accuracy / Crit chance | Rapiers, Daggers, Swords |
| **Agility (AGI)** | Attack speed / Evasion | Daggers, Rapiers |
| **Intelligence (INT)** | Skill effectiveness | Special Weapon Skills |
| **Vitality (VIT)** | Total Health Points (HP) | Everyone (Frontline focus) |
| **Endurance (END)** | Stamina Points (SP) | Heavy weapon users |

Beyond individual stats, your **Cardinal Rank** serves as a global indicator of your progress. This rank dictates the quality of items available for purchase and the difficulty of the monsters you encounter.

<Callout type="success" title="The Cardinal Rank Benefit">

</Callout>
