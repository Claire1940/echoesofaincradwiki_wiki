Mastering the combat flow of the two-handed sword involves more than just pressing your skill buttons on cooldown. Because of the long recovery animations associated with heavy attacks, you must learn to cancel animations and manage your stamina efficiently. Running out of stamina mid-swing leaves you completely defenseless.

Positioning and timing are your primary defensive tools. Since you cannot block with a shield, you must rely on perfect dodges, parries, and spatial awareness to avoid taking damage.
