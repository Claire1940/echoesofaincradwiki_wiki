<Section icon={Target} title="Why Early Daggers Underperform">

A close examination of individual **echoes of aincrad daggers** reveals why they struggle to compete with swords. The game's combat mechanics heavily favor animation canceling and stagger locking, two areas where daggers fail to deliver efficient results.

### The Problem with Dagger Mods

Many daggers feature modifiers that look promising on paper but fail to translate into actual combat efficiency. For example, the Iron Tooth increases normal attack speed by roughly 25% to 30%, but this bonus makes it incredibly difficult to guard or parry effectively.
